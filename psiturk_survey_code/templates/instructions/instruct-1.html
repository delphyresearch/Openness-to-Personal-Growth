Instructions

PLEASE READ ALL INSTRUCTIONS - ONLY COMPLETED SURVEYS WILL BE CREDITED

- In this HIT, you will be asked to complete a personality survey.

- The questions use sliders; they will turn green when you click on them.

- Click anywhere on the slider- the indicator will move to where you've clicked. Alternatively, you can drag the indicator along.

- To leave a slider in the middle, just click on it.

- The sliders are set up so that if you need to, you can go past the left most or right most label - see the example at the bottom of this page.

- The first 100 questions are a new variation of a standard personality survey.

-The last 20 or so questions measure a few new psychological constructs.

-Your answer choices will change at various points in the survey

-You must answer all questions to be credited. The only optional question is the comment box at the very end.

-The website powering this survey can detected random and psychologically impossible answers-such results will not be credited. You must answer all questions to be paid for this HIT.

-There are no 'right' or 'wrong' answers. Just answer according to how you act.
