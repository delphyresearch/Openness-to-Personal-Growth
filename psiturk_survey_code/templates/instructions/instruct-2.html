Instructions

The first 100 questions are a new variation of a standard personality survey. The last 20 or so questions measure a few new psychological constructs.

The last 20 or so questions may contain statements you disagree with or find controversial. Please mark the answers that best reflect you and your opinions.

If a particular statement seems to apply to you more or less than the labels provide, just move the slider all the way to the appropriate end.

There are no 'right' or 'wrong' answers-Just answer according to how you act.

At the very end, you will be asked to provide your age and your gender. There will be an optional box for comments too.
